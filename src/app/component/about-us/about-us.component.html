Welcome to DS Projects Consult

30 Years of Experience in Industry

D S Projects Consult is a civil engineering construction and consultancy firm with an experience of twenty years dedicated to providing high-quality services to clients in various sectors. With a strong team of experienced engineers, architects, project managers, and consultants, we deliver comprehensive solutions for residential, commercial, mixed development and industrial projects of all scales.

Our Services

Structural Engineering

Our structural engineering services encompass the design and analysis of buildings and other structures. We employ analysis tools and techniques to develop safe, efficient, and cost-effective structural solutions.

Project Management

We offer end-to-end project management services, ensuring seamless coordination between various stakeholders, managing timelines, budgets, and resources efficiently. Our dedicated project managers ensure successful project delivery from initiation to completion.

Construction Supervision

We provide comprehensive construction supervision services, overseeing every aspect of the construction process to ensure compliance with design specifications, quality standards, and safety regulations. Our onsite engineers monitor progress, manage subcontractors, and resolve any issues promptly.

Design and Planning

Our team of architects, engineers, and designers collaborate to create innovative and sustainable designs for all the projects.

Building Plan Approval

As registered engineers in the BBMP, we offer clients, Building plan approval services and clearances to start construction activities at sites.
